Department of Computer Science & Engineering

NBA Accredited | Established in 2007

Our Vision

"To be a globally recognized center for imparting quality technical education through innovative research and incubation with moral values in the field of computer science and engineering."

Department Overview

The Department of Computer Science & Engineering was started in the year 2007 with an initial student intake of 60 to cater to software industries. Over the years, the intake capacity has progressively scaled up to 240 seats to meet growing academic demands. The CSE program is proudly accredited by the National Board of Accreditation (NBA).
