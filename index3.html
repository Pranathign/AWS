Department Overview

The Department of Computer Science & Engineering was started in the year 2007 with an initial student intake of 60. Over the years the intake has increased to meet growing industry demands. The department focuses on quality education, research, innovation and industry collaboration. The CSE program is accredited by the National Board of Accreditation (NBA).
